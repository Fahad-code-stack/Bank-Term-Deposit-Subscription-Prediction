Why Random Forest was selected

Random Forest was selected as the deployment model because it provided strong predictive performance, stable results, fast inference time, and good generalization on unseen data. Although the ANN achieved slightly higher accuracy, Random Forest offers simpler deployment, lower computational requirements, and better interpretability, making it more suitable for this project.
