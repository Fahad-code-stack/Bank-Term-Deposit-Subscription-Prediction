Project Information

Overview

This system is designed to predict customer subscription behavior for bank term deposits using a trained Random Forest Classification model. By analyzing demographic variables (age, job, marital status, education) and financial variables (account balance, credit defaults, loans), the system enables financial institutions to optimize their outbound direct marketing campaigns, reduce operational costs, and maximize conversion rates.
